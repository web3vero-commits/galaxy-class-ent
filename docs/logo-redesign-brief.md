# Galaxy Class Entertainment — Logo Redesign Brief

**Goal:** Replace current mark with a clean, sharp, corporate identity that signals authority in the music industry — peer-level with major management firms and labels.

---

## Brand Positioning
- **Not:** indie, edgy, underground, retro
- **Yes:** professional, authoritative, premium, scalable
- Peer brands to benchmark: CAA, WME, Interscope, 300 Entertainment, AWAL

---

## Logo Concepts (3 Directions)

### Direction 1 — "Signal" (Recommended)
**Concept:** The letter G formed from a clean signal/soundwave arc — one bold stroke that implies transmission and reach. No clutter. Monogram-first lockup.

- **Wordmark:** "GALAXY CLASS" in tight, wide-tracked geometric sans (Inter, Neue Haas Grotesk, or custom)
- **Tagline line:** "ENTERTAINMENT" in lighter weight, smaller caps, tracked wide below
- **Mark:** Abstract G / arc — works as standalone app icon or embossed
- **Colors:** Deep space black `#0A0A0F` + electric platinum `#E8E8F0` + single accent: deep cobalt `#1B3FCC` or signal gold `#C9A84C`
- **Vibe:** SpaceX meets Sony Music

---

### Direction 2 — "Constellation" 
**Concept:** "GALAXY CLASS" set in a sharp, slightly condensed serif (Canela, Freight Display, or Editorial New). The G letterform contains a minimal star cluster — 3–5 dots arranged like a constellation. Clean, aspirational, luxury.

- **Wordmark:** Editorial serif, strong weight
- **Mark:** G with constellation dot cluster inside counter space
- **Colors:** Midnight navy `#050A1A` + warm gold `#B8922A` + cream `#F5F0E8`
- **Vibe:** Roc Nation meets a luxury hotel group

---

### Direction 3 — "GCE Monogram"
**Concept:** Tight stacked monogram — G/C/E interlocked or nested. Bold geometric construction, no decorative elements. Works at any size from favicon to billboard. Purely typographic.

- **Wordmark:** "GALAXY CLASS ENTERTAINMENT" in extended, ultra-light weight sans set in all-caps on two lines
- **Mark:** Geometric GCE monogram — think a modern legal/consulting firm logo applied to entertainment
- **Colors:** Pure black + white (primary) + one accent — electric teal `#00D4C8` as differentiator
- **Vibe:** McKinsey meets a modern record label

---

## Typography System (Site-Wide)
Pair with the new logo for full brand consistency:

| Role | Font | Weight |
|------|------|--------|
| Display / Hero | Neue Haas Grotesk Display or Inter Display | 700–900 |
| Body | Inter or DM Sans | 400, 500 |
| Accent / Quote | Editorial New or Canela | 300 italic |
| Mono / Code | JetBrains Mono | 400 |

All available via Google Fonts or Fontshare (free for commercial use).
